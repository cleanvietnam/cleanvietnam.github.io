No Litter Campaign


We are commited to creating a clean environment for future generations


Our action plan, we plan to target different age demographics at different stages of our campaign. Initally we want to raise awareness in children of ages 4-16 through an educational video. It will be distributed via social media and be promoted with partnerships with activist groups in Hanoi. Adolescents of ages 16-25 will then be targeted through a social media awareness campaign. Adults above the age of 25 will be targeted through an educational website.

Benefits of a responsible rubbish disposal


CHANGE: Help create a cleaner environment for people, wildlife and society. The change we can create today will have positive influences for future generations. Otherwise damages can be irreversible and pernamently damage the Earth.


BENEFITS: Avoid: 8 million tonnes of plastic is dumped into the ocean each year by humans. By 2030 for every 1 tonne of plastic there will be 3 tonnes of fish. 80% of ocean waste comes from land. 29% of waste is not collected in urban areas of Vietnam. As plastics degrades into microplastics it will be eaten by planton or krill which will eventually propagate through the food chain. Some plastics are able to retain their recognizeable form 400 years after disposal in the ocean.

TEACHING

Teach your children about:

Picking up rubbish

Raising awareness in children and giving them the responsibility to pick up their own rubbish is good for fostering character.

Recycling duty

Reinforce recycling with rewards to help children understand the benefits of recycling.

Responsible consumption
